Our Process

At ARCHĒ, design begins with exploration — a deliberate process of inquiry, observation, and refinement that shapes every project.

We draw insight from our clients, the context, and our international experience, weaving together elements that are rational and intuitive, cultural and spatial, conceptual and built.

Our process is rooted in listening, not only to what is articulated, but also to what is revealed through place, behavior, and constraint. Through close collaboration, we develop a design language unique to each project.

Guiding Design Principles


Every ARCHĒ project responds to a specific set of conditions, site, program, culture, and ambition. What connects our work is a shared philosophy: a commitment to clarity, balance, and purpose.
These principles guide our decisions from concept through execution, ensuring that each project is both grounded in context and distinct in character.

01. Context

Architecture begins with place. We approach every project through a deep understanding of its physical, cultural, and social context. Climate, history, scale, and local patterns of use inform our design decisions, allowing each project to emerge as a natural extension of its environment rather than an imposition upon it. By grounding design in context, we create spaces that feel authentic, relevant, and enduring.

02. Composition

Composition is the discipline that gives form to intention. We design through proportion, rhythm, and material relationships, carefully balancing structure and openness, solidity and lightness. Each space is considered as part of a larger whole, ensuring continuity and coherence across the project. Through thoughtful composition, architecture becomes legible, calm, and purposeful.

03. Emotion

Architecture is experienced before it is understood. We design with an awareness of how space is perceived, felt, and remembered. Light, texture, scale, and sequence are orchestrated to create atmospheres that engage the senses and shape emotional response. Our goal is to create environments that resonate — spaces that offer comfort, inspiration, and a sense of belonging.

04. Delight

Delight emerges through precision. Beyond function and form, we look for moments that elevate the everyday — subtle gestures, unexpected details, and refined transitions that reward attention and use. These moments create lasting impressions, transforming spaces into experiences and architecture into memory.
